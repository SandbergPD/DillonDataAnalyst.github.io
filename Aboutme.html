Dillon Sandberg

Data Analyst

Dedicated and adaptable professional with a background in electrical contracting and surveillance system analysis, poised to transition into a dynamic role as a Data Analyst. Possessing a robust technical skill set including SQL, Tableau, Microsoft Excel, R Programming, and Python, combined with a keen eye for data presentation and visualization, I am adept at harnessing data to derive actionable insights and drive strategic decision-making.

In my role as Lead Apprentice Electrician at JMEG Electrical Contractors, I honed invaluable skills in problem-solving and database management, driving a 25% increase in team productivity by streamlining workflows and providing mentorship to foster professional growth. My ability to assess costs, scope of work, and communicate complex concepts to non-technical stakeholders underscores my capacity to translate technical data into meaningful business solutions.

Transitioning to the role of Surveillance Systems Analyst at Supernova Networks, I demonstrated proficiency in project management, risk assessment, and issue tracking, resulting in a 60% reduction in nuisance alarm events through the design of innovative filters and meticulous analysis of activity logs. Moreover, my capacity to align camera environments with customer requirements exemplifies my aptitude for tailoring data solutions to meet specific business needs.

Complementing my practical experience, I have augmented my skills through formal education, earning a Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate and completing coursework in data visualization. Additionally, I have engaged in immersive job simulations to further refine my abilities in leveraging data to empower businesses with actionable insights.

Driven by a passion for leveraging data to drive strategic outcomes, I am eager to apply my diverse skill set and proven track record of success to excel in a Data Analyst role, contributing to the optimization of processes, enhancement of decision-making frameworks, and ultimately, the achievement of organizational objectives.
